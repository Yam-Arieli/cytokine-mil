**Reading:** this tree is a pure *retention* structure (descendant carries
ancestor), so ‚Äî consistent with the ¬ß32 state-axis finding ‚Äî `cross_asym` mis-signs
**all four** ancestor‚Üídescendant edges (each names the descendant as upstream) while
leaving the two sibling pairs correctly ambiguous (‚âà0). The reversal magnitude tracks
the carry strength: the direct parent‚Üíchild edges (p0‚Üíp1, p1‚Üíp2, p0‚Üíp3, carry 0.5m)
give |cross_asym| ‚âà 0.61, the transitive grandparent edge p0‚Üíp2 (carry 0.25m) gives a
weaker 0.26. So the tree reproduces the `monotone_noseed` regime (0% / œÑ=‚àí1) on a
non-linear, partial-order topology ‚Äî confirming the reversal is about the
acquisition-vs-retention asymmetry, not about the chain being linear.
